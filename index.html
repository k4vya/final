Top 3 Symptoms to Watch Out For

Please remember that symptoms can take from 2-14 days to show themselves

Fever

Fevers are classified as bodily temperatures of above normal (37C)

Cough

Coronavirus coughs are characteristically dry, not wet.

Shortness of Breath

This is one of the most important symptoms to watch out for. Shortness of breath can affect people with pre-existing respitory symptoms.

Ways to Avoid Catching & Spreading the Virus

Listed below are some of the most important ways you can ensure you don't catch the virus or spread it to the people around you

  • Don't touch Face
    Avoid touching your face, nose, mouth, or ears during the day. Doing so can spread the virus much easier into your body!
  • Wash your hands often
    The virus can stay alive for days on various surfaces including plastics and metals, so washing your hands often is a key way to combat this! Scroll up for more information.
  • Social Distancing is Key
    Coronavirus infects exponentially, so making sure you stay away from other people is important in order to flatten the curve of infection. Work from home, avoid public settings and large gatherings, and only leave the home when absolutely necessary, such as to buy groceries. If you are in a public space for an essential reason, keep 6+ feet of space from others.
